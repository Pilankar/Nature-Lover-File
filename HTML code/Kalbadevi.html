Beach

Kalbadevi Beach



    Kalbadevi beach is one of the best beach in Ratnagiri. It is the better place to spend time.

Special Features :-

  • Beach is clean as you can see in picture.
  • The best part is there is no fear of drawning like other beaches. You can go 6-7 metres inside without any fear.
  • One of the best place for the nature lover as there is greenary everywhere
  • You will get feeling of wavepool here.
  • Here you can see one of the best sunsets.
  • It's not a crowdy place.
  • If you want, you can collect seashells from there.

Location :-


For the better peace of mind, you can also visit Rameshwar Temple located just some metres away from this beach.

    There are no food stalls, like shevpuri or panipuri, as this place is in village. But you can take some snacks or food with yourself as we do in picnics.

    Unfortunately there are no changing rooms. But if you want, you can change your clothes in the washroom located just near the Rameshwar Temple.


    So, if you are getting bore, this is the best place to spend time with yourself, family, children, date or friends. No need to travel abroad or take flights and spend lots of money only just for enjoying beach. Come in the Kalbadevi Beach and spend time with your loveones.
